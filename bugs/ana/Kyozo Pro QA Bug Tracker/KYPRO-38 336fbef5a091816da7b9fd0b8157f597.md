Steps to reproduce: Scenario A – Intra-batch duplicate (original):
1. Navigate to Audience > Import Members > Manual Entry
2. Enter a contact – e.g. Name: "Alice Tester", Email: mailto:alice@test.com – and click + Add
3. Enter the exact same name and email again
4. Click + Add again
5. Observe: both rows appear with no duplicate warning

Scenario B – Cross-audience duplicate (confirmed 2026-04-08):
1. Ensure a member with email mailto:alice@example.com already exists in the Audience
2. Navigate to Audience > Import Members > Manual Entry
3. Enter Name: "Alice Johnson", Email: mailto:alice@example.com
4. Click + Add, then click 'Import 1 Members'
5. Observe: import succeeds silently; a second member with the same email appears in Audience
